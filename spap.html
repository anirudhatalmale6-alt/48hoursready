About SPAP

Secteur Prive des Affaires Populaire

SPAP (Secteur Prive des Affaires Populaire) is a strategic business network operating inside the 48HoursReady ecosystem, dedicated to helping Haiti's entrepreneurs, small business owners, and informal workers transition into structured, sustainable businesses. SPAP provides the bridge between informal economic activity and formal business participation.


Through training, mentorship, formalization support, and direct connection to the HaitiBiznis ecosystem, SPAP empowers entrepreneurs at every stage. Whether you run a market stall, a local service, a growing startup, or a community business - SPAP gives you the tools, network, and visibility to grow with confidence. Together, we're building a more structured and inclusive economy for Haiti.
